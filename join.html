JOIN WVAC

We are glad you are considering serving your neighbors here in Westwood as a member of the Westwood Volunteer Ambulance Corps, Inc. WVAC is always looking for more members to join. We welcome anyone who wants to ride with us and help us serve Westwood. We welcome EMTs and EMRs to join, but you do not need to be either to start riding with us.

Eligibility

To be a volunteer, applicants must meet the following minimum requirements:

  1. Is the age of 16 or older
  2. Lives or works in Westwood, or lives within a 3 mile radius of the building

Email us at Join@WestwoodVolunteerEMS.org or call us at (201) 664-0003 to join. A meeting will be scheduled with our recruiting committee before joining. Please download our Application for Membership Form and complete it before contacting us. We look forward to having you join!

Education

Once again, you do not need to be an EMT to join, but if you are interested in becoming one we will assist you through your education.
