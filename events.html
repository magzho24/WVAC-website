EVENTS

The Westwood Volunteer Ambulance Corps not only does a great service for the Pascack Valley through its emergency services, it is also a fun organization to be apart of!

Every year members of the corps and their families participate in corps events including our annual BBQ, Installation Dinner, and Holiday Brunch. The corps also works together to run the annual WVAC Carnival and other fundraising events.

The corps also participates in town events including the Memorial Day Parade, Home for the Holidays and Westwood Day.
