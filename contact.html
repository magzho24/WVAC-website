Equipment Loans

â€‹Since 1935 when the first ambulance responded in Westwood, one of the extra services WVAC have always provided to community of Westwood is our Medical Equipment Loan Program. We recycle medical equipment that is not longer needed by Westwood families and make that equipment available to residents in need. All equipment is available at no fee.

To borrow crutches, wheelchairs, walkers, canes, or commodes, please call our non-emergency number, 201-664-0003 and leave a message for our Equipment Loan Coordinator.

If you have medical equipment that you are returning or no longer need, simply leave it outside our garage, located on the eastern end of the Westwood Municipal Complex at 101 Washington Ave.
