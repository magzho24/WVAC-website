SUPPORT US AND DONATE

It is through the ongoing support of the citizens of Westwood and our municipal government that the Westwood Volunteer Ambulance Corps is able to provide emergency medical services at not cost to the Borough of Westwood and its surrounding towns. The ambulance corps runs strictly on donations, without using any taxpayer money. We are primarily dependent upon your generosity to purchase equipment, supplies and education/training for our volunteer members. Your support helps keeps WVAC running.

We gladly accept and appreciate monetary donations. All donations are tax deductible, and can be made through PayPal (credit card) or by check.
